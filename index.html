The gap

The same clients. Two very different standards.

The luxury industry and financial services court the very same high-net-worth individuals — yet they train their people to entirely different standards. A private banker and a maison's clienteling director may sit across from the same client in the same week. Only one has been taught to earn a relationship that lasts decades.

That gap is quiet, but it is expensive. It costs firms the trust, the loyalty and the referrals that the world's great luxury houses treat as a discipline. This preview shows you exactly where that gap lives in your own team — and what closing it is worth.
